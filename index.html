Janitorial Checklist

Daily Tasks

Restroom

Clean and sanitize all restrooms
Thoroughly mop, clean and sanitize all floor surfaces
Empty, clean and dispose of all waste and trash baskets
Check and restock if necessary towels, toilet tissue, face tissue and hand soap
Thoroughly scrub toilet bowls and urinals

Treatment Room, Waiting Room, Offices, Staff Lounge and Hallways

Clean and sanitize all surfaces excluding dialysis machines and chairs
Vacuum all carpeted areas
Thoroughly mop, clean and sanitize all floor surfaces
Clean all interior glass surfaces
Empty, clean and dispose all non-infectious, non-hazardous waste paper baskets, trash conatiners and smoking receptacles
Dust or clean with damp cloth furniture, fixture, telephone, cabinets, files and counters
Spot clean all walls, doors, door frames, partitions to remove all finderprints or markings
Clean all drinking fountains, water coolers and outer surface of refridgerator
Check and restock if necessary all hygiene material

Isolation Area

Wearing PPE and using cleaning equipment designed for the isolation area, sanitize all surfaces excluding dialysis machine and chairs

Stockroom

Sweep
Remove broken down boxes

General Floor Care

Sweeping, mopping and vacuuming of all floor surface area

Weekly Tasks

Treatment Room, Waiting Room, Offices, Staff Lounge and Hallways

Dust windowsills, blinds, low ledges, baseboards, waiting room chair rungs, picture frames and all other wall-mounted items
Clean and sanitze telephones

Stockroom

Dry mop

Dumpster

Clean up litter overflow around dumpster

Monthly Tasks

Entire Facility

Dust and clean all air vents
Dust high moldings and ledges
Dust all light fixtures
Clean interior side of all glass windows

Report issues
